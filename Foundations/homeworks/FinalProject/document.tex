\section{Modifying Section 2's Equation}
\frame{
\frametitle{Modifying Question 2's Equations}
By introducing a death rate, we must now modify our differential equations: 
\begin{align*}
    S'(t) &= -a S I \\
    I'(t) &= a S I + b R - r I - c I\\
    R'(t) &= r I - b R\\
    D'(t) &= c I
\end{align*}

}
